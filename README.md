# 📊 Sales & Profit Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a **Sales & Profit Performance Analysis Dashboard** built using Tableau.  
The goal of this analysis is to identify key business insights from retail sales data, including sales trends, regional performance, customer segment contribution, and product category profitability.

The dashboard helps businesses understand **which regions, segments, and products drive revenue and profit**, enabling better strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze monthly profit trends.
- Identify top-performing states by sales.
- Understand profit contribution by customer segment.
- Examine the relationship between sales and profit.
- Compare category performance across regions.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 1️⃣ Monthly Profit Trend
The line chart shows how profit changes across different months, highlighting seasonal trends and profit fluctuations.

### 2️⃣ Top 5 State-wise Sales
This visualization identifies the states generating the highest sales revenue.

### 3️⃣ Segment-wise Profit
The chart compares profits generated from different customer segments such as Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office.

### 4️⃣ Sales vs Profit Analysis
A scatter plot is used to analyze the relationship between sales and profit, helping identify high-sales but low-profit scenarios.

### 5️⃣ Region vs Category Profit
A heatmap shows how different product categories perform across various regions.

### 6️⃣ Region vs Sales
This chart compares total sales generated in each region.
